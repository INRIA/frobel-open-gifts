Welcome to Frobel-open-gifts Pages.

Fröbel Open Gift is probably one of the first open source hardware kit for education in kindergarten. Fröbel Open Gift is an open source version of the Friedrich Fröbel pedagogical material. Over the ten original Gifts we have just re designed the tiles buidling block from Gift 7. Two version are accessibles, the 3d printable and a laser cuttable

The kit has been already used in many workshop during the last two year.

Froebel Open Gift 7 A and B

We've crafted a handsome box for you to store your tails. The box 7A store 144 tokens of various colors 36 orange, 36 purple, 36 yellow and 36 black tiles. And the box 7B store also 144 tokens of different colors: 36 red, 36 green, 36 blue and 36 white tiles.

Roadmap

We want to

  • * improve the cost
  • * improve the design of the box
  • * make a tutorial to help people reproduce it by themself
